I feel as though it should be a more popular sport in the United States. I was also able to see the Phoenix Coyotes play several times.

The last thing I have been developing an increasing interest in is politics. I feel as though a healthy system is vital for the maintenance and development of our society. I think that it is important to monitor trends and have a general understanding of history. This, I feel, is the most basic duty of a citizen. I feel we have a sacred duty to provide a future world for our next generations. I feel that we must sacrifice and make difficult decisions to make this possible. We should leave the world for our kids better than we found it.
